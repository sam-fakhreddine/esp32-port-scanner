The Vulnerability:

  • Device: Implanted cardiac pacemakers (St. Jude/Abbott)
  • Flaw: Wireless RF telemetry could be hijacked
  • Threat: Unauthorized reprogramming in close proximity
  • Potential Impact: Rapid battery depletion or altered pacing shocks
  • Fix: Firmware update (required doctor visit)

First-ever FDA recall of medical devices for cybersecurity vulnerabilities

Lesson: Life-critical IoT devices can have life-threatening vulnerabilities. Medical IoT security is literally life-or-death.
