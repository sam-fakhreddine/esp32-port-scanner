The Attack Chain:

  • Entry Point: Fazio Mechanical Services (HVAC contractor)
  • Vector: Stolen vendor credentials for network access
  • Lateral Movement: Moved from vendor access to point-of-sale systems
  • Payload: Malware installed during 2013 holiday shopping season
  • Impact: CEO resigned, brand reputation damaged

Lesson: Your security is only as strong as your weakest third-party vendor. Supply-chain risk is real.
