When the Human is the Weakest Link

2023
Both Breached
Vishing
Phone-Based Attack
$100M+
Estimated Losses

MGM Resorts Attack:

  • Attackers impersonated an employee via phone call
  • Tricked IT help-desk into resetting privileged account
  • Deployed ransomware across casino systems
  • Slot machines, room keys, and reservations down for days

Caesars Entertainment Attack:

  • Social engineering via outsourced IT support vendor
  • Reportedly paid ransom to prevent data leak

Lesson: Third-party human factors (help-desk, contractors, call centers) are part of your attack surface. MFA isn't enough when attackers can just call your help desk.
