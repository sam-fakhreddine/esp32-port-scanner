When Your Aquarium Betrays You

Attack Vector: Internet-connected aquarium thermometer
Location: Luxury casino lobby
Target: High-roller customer database
Exfiltration: Through the thermostat to cloud storage
Detection: Darktrace AI noticed unusual data flow

How It Happened:

  • Smart thermostat had vulnerability in firmware
  • Device connected to main casino network (no segmentation)
  • Attackers exploited IoT device as entry point
  • Accessed database and exfiltrated via same device
  • Data sent "up to the cloud" through thermostat's connection

Lesson: Even a $50 fish tank thermometer can compromise a multi-million dollar casino. IoT devices expand your attack surface exponentially.
