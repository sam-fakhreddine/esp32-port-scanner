Sacred Rituals to Protect Your Digital Soul

Immediate Rituals:

  • Network Segmentation - Separate VLAN for IoT
  • Change Defaults - Every single device
  • Disable Services - Turn off Telnet, FTP, UPnP

Ongoing Protection Spells:

  • Update Firmware - Quarterly reminders
  • Monitor Network - Know what's connected
  • Replace Old Devices - If it can't update, replace it

The best defense is awareness. You can't protect what you don't know exists.
